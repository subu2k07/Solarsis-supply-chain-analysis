# Solarsis-supply-chain-analysis
Analyzed  ~20,000 supply-chain orders for a fictional company (Solarsis) to identify operational risk hidden behoind a successful OTD rate of 87.3% and strategic recommendations.
A consulting and strategy recruitment case focused on a renewable energy company experiencing delivery-performance issues despite achieving its overall OTD target.

OBJECTIVE:

Identify hidden operational risks and recommend strategic recommendation to solve the poor operational failures.


BUSINESS PROBLEM:
Solarsis overall OTD rate was 87.3%, above the targeted 80% set by the company.
but, with a closer look, it was experiencing delivery delays across customer segments, logistic partners, products and factories.

key questions:
1. which logistic partners are underperforming and why?
2. which product have the highest operational risk?
3. which factory is contributing the most to delays?
4. what is the consequence of poor delkivery performance in revenue?
5. how to manage these operational fauilures?

METHODOLOGY:
I used kaggle notebook to perform my analysis.

1.DELIVERY PARTNER ANALYSIS:
-calculated OTD rates
-compared performance against targeted OTD
-estimated revenue at risk

2.PRODUCT ANALYSIS:
-evaluated delivery performance by product category
-assesed revenue contribution
-identified operational risks

3.CUSTOMER SEGMENT ANALYSIS
-measured OTD performance across customer segments
-assesed revenue exposure from delivery failures

4.FACTORY ANALYSIS
-compared factory level delivery performance
-identified potential capacity and efficiency concerns
